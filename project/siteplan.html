When I started the company I wanted to create a name connected to something strong and masculine. Titan came up as strong and masculine and toolin' connects it to the construction side of things.

Toolin' Titan: Handyman Services is a handyman company made for any of those little construction projects that contractors don't care to take care of. This site is a place to learn more about the handyman, the services we provide, and a form to submit a request for a quote.
